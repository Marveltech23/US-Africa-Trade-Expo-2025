Who We Are

About Us

The US-Africa Trade Expo is a premier platform dedicated to fostering strong business collaborations between African trade experts and their counterparts in the United States. Scheduled to take place in the vibrant city of Lagos, Nigeria, this transformative conference is designed to bridge continents, unlock opportunities, and shape the future of international trade.

Our Vision

To create a dynamic ecosystem where African innovation and expertise meet US markets and technology, paving the way for global trade excellence and sustainable economic growth.

Our Mission

Through impactful business ideologies, collaborations, and strategic connections, the US-Africa Trade Expo aims to: Showcase Africa‚Äôs trade expertise and potential. Highlight cutting-edge business solutions from both continents. Build lasting partnerships for mutual prosperity.

Our Expo Agenda

Get ready for an action-packed day of insights and opportunities at our US-Africa Trade Expo. We've curated a program filled with engaging sessions to spark your interest as far as innovation and collaboration.is concerned, some of which include:


Diplomatic and Bilateral Trade Roundtable

Get ready to explore the role of diplomacy in shaping US-Africa trade relations. Discuss key trade agreements, diplomatic strategies, and government policies that influence bilateral commerce.


Educational and Job Opportunity Expo

We will connect talent with opportunity, and explore educational programs, scholarships, and job prospects available in both regions.


Trade, Investment, and Innovation Exchange Expo

The power of innovation will be unleashed, aimed at discovering next-generation solutions, exploring business ventures, and networking with potential partners.

Energy Roundtable

Be a part of the system that shapes the future of energy trade and investment. Join discussions on the latest trends and challenges, explore renewable energy opportunities, and collaborate on strategies for sustainable practices.


Investment Roundtable

With this, there will be the opportunity to connect with investors, financiers, and entrepreneurs. Gain valuable insights into funding opportunities, learn effective investment strategies, and network with key players in the finance and investment ecosystem.


Export/Import Roundtable

Unravel the complexities of US-Africa trade. Discuss solutions to overcome obstacles like infrastructure gaps, customs complexities, and regulatory hurdles. Identify ways to improve market access and explore the potential of technology in streamlining trade processes.
